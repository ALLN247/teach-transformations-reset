## What This Is

The **15-Minute AI Teacher ResET** is a free, open-source web tool built for urban educators in under-resourced school districts.

It generates ready-to-use AI prompts — for lesson planning, differentiation, grading, student engagement, sub plans, and assessments — in under 15 minutes. No AI account required to start. No cost. No gatekeeping. Ever.

This is not an AI that replaces teachers. This is infrastructure that gives teachers superpowers.

---

## Who Built This & Why

I'm **Chazmen Geames** — Coach G. I teach Earth Science, Applied Physics, Anatomy & Physiology, and Health & Wellness to 200+ students across four preparations at UPrep Charter School in Rochester, NY. One of the most under-resourced urban districts in America.

I built this because I watched wealthy districts hand their teachers AI training, premium tools, and time — while urban educators like me were expected to do more with less, every single day.

That's not Fair Play.

So I built the Batcave myself. And then I opened the doors.

## How To Use It

1. **Open `reset-tool.html`** in any browser — no installation needed
2. **Enter your grade level, subject, and topic**
3. **Choose your tool** (lesson plan, grading, engagement, etc.)
4. **Copy the generated prompt**
5. **Paste into Claude, ChatGPT, or Gemini** (all free)
6. **Get your output** — done in under 15 minutes

Zero backend. Zero account. Zero cost.
